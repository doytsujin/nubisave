Join us

NubiSave started out as a practical team lab project at the Faculty of Computer Science, Technische Universität Dresden. The lab project was supervised by Josef Spillner and implemented by Gerd Bombach, Steffen Matthischke, Johannes Müller and Rico Tzschichholz. It is now continued here as an open source project and welcomes contributions.
